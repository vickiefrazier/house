Calling All Crafters

I am providing, at a minimal cost to you, a place for you to show off your quality work.  Craft items, quilted projects, quilts, jewelry, knitted projects, stained glass, Kits containing a pattern you created and materials to complete the project.

Almost anything goes.  For customers to trust my site and keep coming back they must to know that the merchandise is of the highest quality.  So the only requirement is that any finished items have to meet a high standard of quality.

For you, my friends and quilt guild members, there will be no charge for setting you up on my site.  I will collect a 10% fee of the sales price for the first $500 per month and 1% of all amounts over $500.00. If you are selling a one time item then I will send your money to you either by pay-pal or by check as soon as I have confirmation that the merchandise has reached the buyer.  If you are a regular seller with continuous merchandise on the site I will cut you a check once a month or if sales are large enough it could be weekly.  Some of these details will be worked out as we go.

When a customer has purchased your product I will contact you either by e-mail or by phone (if you don't use a computer).  You will ship your merchandise to the customer and use some kind of tracking system.  If this is unclear I will explain it to you when you are ready to place something on the site to sell.

I need suggestions and input. Any help you can give will be greatly appreciated.  Questions are always helpful, it may be something I haven't thought about.
