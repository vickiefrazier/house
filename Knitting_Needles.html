Hand Crafted Wood Knitting Needles

Regular store bought needles are 14" long. The length caused me to have to lift the needles to a point that caused my shoulders to hurt. I searched throughout my town and neighboring towns for shorter needles but could not find any. The necessity for shorter needles led me to develop these wood needles. I discovered that the wood was also better on my hands, it did not get cold like metal needles do. The wood also gave me more control and I could knit faster. I believe you will enjoy these wood needles as much as I have.
